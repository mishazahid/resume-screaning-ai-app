Data Scientist
Location: Remote (US) | Full-Time
Company: Meridian Analytics

About the Role
--------------
Meridian Analytics is a B2B SaaS company helping mid-market companies make faster,
evidence-based decisions. We are hiring a Data Scientist to join our growing Data
Intelligence team. You will own the full modelling lifecycle — from data exploration
and feature engineering through to deploying models that power our product's
recommendation and forecasting features.

Responsibilities
----------------
- Design, build, and validate supervised and unsupervised machine learning models
  using Python and scikit-learn to solve real customer problems.
- Use SQL to query, join, and transform large relational datasets for model training,
  evaluation, and ad-hoc business analysis.
- Apply statistics best practices — hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, and
  regression analysis — to validate experiments and communicate uncertainty.
- Collaborate with the product and engineering teams to productionise models and
  integrate outputs into customer-facing features.
- Work with pandas and numpy to build efficient data-preparation and feature pipelines.
- Present findings, model performance metrics, and trade-offs clearly to both
  technical peers and non-technical stakeholders.

Required Qualifications
-----------------------
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
- 3+ years of experience in a data science or applied machine learning role.
- Strong Python skills with hands-on experience using scikit-learn and pandas.
- Solid understanding of statistics: distributions, hypothesis testing, and model
  evaluation metrics (precision, recall, AUC, RMSE).
- Proficiency with SQL for data extraction and transformation.
- Familiarity with version control (Git) and working in an agile environment.

Preferred Qualifications
------------------------
- Master's degree in a quantitative discipline.
- Experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch or TensorFlow).
- Hands-on experience with large-scale data processing using Apache Spark.
- Cloud experience on AWS (S3, SageMaker) or GCP.
- Familiarity with data visualisation tools such as Tableau or Plotly.

What We Offer
-------------
- Competitive salary ($110 K–$145 K depending on experience)
- Fully remote with quarterly team off-sites
- $3,000 annual learning budget
- Generous health, dental, and vision benefits

Meridian Analytics is an equal-opportunity employer committed to building
an inclusive and diverse team.
