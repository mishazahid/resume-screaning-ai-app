Senior Data Scientist
Location: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid) | Full-Time
Company: Nexus Analytics

About the Role
--------------
Nexus Analytics is a fast-growing SaaS company helping Fortune 500 clients turn raw data
into competitive advantage. We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our Core
Intelligence team and take ownership of our next-generation recommendation and forecasting
systems. You will work closely with engineering, product, and executive stakeholders to
design, build, and ship machine learning models that directly drive product outcomes.

Responsibilities
----------------
- Design and implement end-to-end machine learning pipelines, from data ingestion and
  feature engineering through to model training, evaluation, and production deployment.
- Collaborate with software engineers to integrate ML models into our platform via
  clean, well-documented APIs (REST API / FastAPI).
- Work with the analytics team to define KPIs, run A/B testing experiments, and
  translate model outputs into actionable business insights presented to stakeholders.
- Maintain and improve existing predictive models by monitoring performance, detecting
  data drift, and conducting scheduled retraining cycles.
- Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to a culture of technical excellence
  through code reviews, documentation, and knowledge-sharing sessions.
- Partner with the data engineering team to build robust, scalable data pipelines using
  pandas, SQL, and Spark.

Required Qualifications
-----------------------
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
- 4+ years of experience in a data science or applied machine learning role.
- Strong programming skills in Python; experience writing production-quality code.
- Solid understanding of supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms;
  hands-on experience with scikit-learn.
- Proficiency in data manipulation and analysis with pandas, numpy, and SQL.
- Strong statistics background: hypothesis testing, probability distributions,
  regression analysis, and time series forecasting.
- Experience using version control (Git / GitHub) and working in an agile / scrum
  environment.

Preferred Qualifications
------------------------
- Master's degree or PhD in a quantitative discipline.
- Experience with deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch or TensorFlow.
- Familiarity with NLP techniques and large language models (LLMs / transformers).
- Experience processing large datasets with Apache Spark or Hadoop.
- Cloud platform experience: AWS (S3, SageMaker, EC2) or GCP (BigQuery, Vertex AI).
- Exposure to MLOps practices: model versioning, monitoring, CI/CD pipelines for ML,
  and containerisation with Docker / Kubernetes.
- Experience with data visualisation tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or Plotly.
